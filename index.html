Through Oxygenate Environmental Consultants

Oxygenating Bamboo — Restoring Air, Restoring Life

eTech Fusion Group champions an initiative, delivered through Oxygenate Environmental Consultants, to scale the planting and cultivation of oxygenating bamboo — one of the most effective and fastest-growing natural carbon sequesters available. Bamboo sequesters up to 12 tonnes of CO² per hectare per year while releasing 35% more oxygen than equivalent forest, improving air quality, preventing soil erosion, and supporting biodiversity.

The initiative connects corporate partners, landowners, and communities to fund, plant, and track bamboo groves — creating verified green corridors that contribute directly to national net-zero targets.

Enerlink.ai — Linking Energy to Intelligence

eTech Fusion Group has a strategic investment in Enerlink.ai, a Swiss-based clean-tech platform that uses AI and blockchain to measure, certify, and monetise emission reductions from clean energy projects worldwide. With over 20,000 projects completed, Enerlink qualifies renewable energy projects via its ISO-accredited Earth Standard — turning fragmented project data into bankable intelligence that unlocks funding at scale.

The platform generates Decarb-certified Emission Reductions (DERs) and Verra-certified VCUs. Recognised at COP30 in Belém, Enerlink presents at the AI for Good Global Summit Geneva 2026.

Overview

A seat at the table
where decisions are made

The WEF27 Davos Trade Envoy Programme grants you access to the same corridor where heads of state, central bank governors, and global CEOs convene — not as an observer, but as a participant.

We host a private dinner within the Davos security zone during the World Economic Forum’s Annual Meeting 2027. Attendance at the dinner serves as your access credential, and security badges are arranged through our allocation — removing one of the most significant barriers to meaningful Davos participation.

This is not a fringe event or a side gathering. This is access inside the perimeter, at the table where global trade, policy, and investment conversations happen. The distinction matters enormously to those who understand the difference.

Crucially, we match your sector focus to the right people in the room. Whether your area is finance, energy, technology, healthcare, or geopolitics, your placement and conversations are directed towards the most relevant decision-makers present.

“Your sector focus determines who you sit with. We ensure your pitch reaches the right ears — so every conversation is a strategic one.”
