Decarbonisation & Green Energy

How eTech Fusion Group supports the transition to a low-carbon future — through the Oxygenating Bamboo Global Programme and strategic investment in green energy innovation.

Our commitment

eTech Fusion Group treats net zero as a core belief, not a compliance exercise. We believe the transition to a low-carbon economy needs both nature-based restoration and AI-powered measurement — working in parallel, at scale, and with integrity. We support that transition in two distinct ways: championing the Oxygenating Bamboo Global Programme, and investing in green-energy innovation.

Oxygenating Bamboo Global Programme

We champion a global initiative, delivered through Oxygenate Environmental Consultants, to scale the planting and cultivation of oxygenating bamboo — one of the most effective and fastest-growing natural carbon sequesters available. Bamboo sequesters up to 12 tonnes of CO² per hectare per year while releasing 35% more oxygen than equivalent forest, improving air quality, preventing soil erosion, and supporting biodiversity.

The programme connects corporate partners, landowners, and communities to fund, plant, and track bamboo groves — creating verified green corridors that contribute directly to national net-zero targets.

Investor in Green Energy — Enerlink.ai

eTech Fusion Group holds a strategic investment in Enerlink.ai, a Swiss-based clean-tech platform that uses AI and blockchain to measure, certify, and monetise emission reductions from clean-energy projects worldwide. With over 20,000 projects completed, Enerlink qualifies renewable-energy projects via its ISO-accredited Earth Standard — turning fragmented project data into bankable intelligence that unlocks funding at scale.

The platform generates Decarb-certified Emission Reductions (DERs) and Verra-certified VCUs. Recognised at COP30 in Belém, Enerlink presents at the AI for Good Global Summit Geneva 2026.
